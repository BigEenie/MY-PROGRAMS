About This Project

Welcome to the {{ settings.university_name }} Course Materials Portal! This platform was created with a passion for making academic resources more accessible to fellow UI students. As a current student at the {{ settings.university_name }}, I understand the challenges of finding and organizing course materials across different faculties and departments.

This portal serves as a centralized hub where students can easily navigate through their faculties, departments, and academic levels to access relevant course materials stored on Google Drive. The goal is to create a seamless, beautiful, and intuitive experience for academic resource discovery.

Motivation

For Students

Making academic resources easily accessible to all UI students, regardless of their technical background.

Mobile-First

Designed with mobile users in mind, ensuring seamless access from any device, anywhere.

Innovation

Bringing modern web technologies to academic resource management at UI.
